EL NIDO

Hover over an image.

Small Lagoon, a charming destination in Miniloc Island, offers beauty in a more intimate setting. Accessible by swimming or kayaking through a crack in a limestone wall, visitors can enjoy the serene turquoise waters surrounded by cliffs. The natural pool provides a peaceful spot for floating or kayaking.

El Nido's Secret Beach, accessible only by swimming through a hole in limestone cliffs, inspired Alex Garland's novel "The Beach" and its film adaptation starring Leonardo DiCaprio. Beware of strong waves; tour guides provide life vests and assistance for this challenging yet rewarding experience.
